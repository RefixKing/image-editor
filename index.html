How to Use This Image Editor
  1. Upload Image: Click the "Choose Image" button to select a picture from your computer.
  2. Apply Edits: Once your image appears, use the controls on the left panel.
    • Filters: Adjust the sliders to change brightness, contrast, blur, and more. Changes are applied live.
    • Resize: Click the "Resize" tab, enter new dimensions, and hit "Apply Resize".
    • Crop: Click "Crop Image", then click and drag a box over your picture. Click "Apply Crop" to finish.
  3. Save Your Work: Click "Download" to save the edited image. Use "Reset" to start over with the original image at any time.
